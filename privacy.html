Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Neon Tic Tac Toe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide while using our app.

Information We Don't Collect

Neon Tic Tac Toe is a simple offline game that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app:

  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not track user location
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not connect to the internet

App Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: Only used to save game settings on your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) as we do not collect any personal information from users of any age.
